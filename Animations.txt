1. - CONCEPTS:
    - Interpolator: Determines the rate of change for an animation f.e: BounceInterpolator,
        AnticipateInterpolator
    - AnimationSet: Group of animations

2. - TYPES:
    - Translate: Move view from one point to other
    - Alpha: Change alpha to change view visibility, fade in or fade out
    - Scale: Change the size of view
    - Rotate: Rotate view
